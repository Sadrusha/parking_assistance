CCTV Monitor



A "CCTV Monitor" is an integral part of a modern parking management system, such as the one implemented by "INNOVKRYPT SOLUTIONS". It provides real-time video surveillance of parking areas, helping ensure the safety and security of both vehicles and individuals. By continuously displaying live footage from strategically placed cameras, the CCTV monitor allows parking operators to monitor available slots, identify any suspicious activities, and manage traffic flow efficiently. Integrated with the digital assistance for guiding vehicles to parking spots, the CCTV system enhances the overall functionality and safety of the parking environment, providing a seamless and secure experience for users.
